FAQ

Common questions

Where does the data come from?
All permit data is pulled directly from county building department public records. These are the same records available at the county office - we automate the process and deliver them weekly.
What does "exclusive" mean exactly?
We only sell one subscription per trade per county. If you're a roofer in Leon County, no other roofer in Leon County receives the same list.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No. Month-to-month. Cancel at any time and your slot opens up for someone else.
How many leads will I get per week?
Depends on the county and season. Busy counties like Hillsborough can generate 100+ permits per week. Smaller counties like Leon typically see 15-40.
What counties do you cover?
Due to high demand and success over the past year, we are rapidly expanding our coverage to new territories. We currently cover Leon, Hillsborough, Polk, and Pasco counties in Florida, with more added monthly. If yours isn't listed, reach out - we can usually add it within a week.
Can I try it before I pay?
Absolutely. Use the contact button to request a free sample. We'll send a full week's worth of data for your county so you can evaluate quality before spending a dime.
